Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, all baked to perfection. It's a hearty and comforting meal perfect for family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24 oz) jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Steps:
  1. Prepare the Noodles: Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the Meat Sauce: In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat. Add the marinara sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture: In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Place 3 noodles on top. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then top with 1/3 of the meat sauce and 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers twice, ending with mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Bake: Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  6. Rest and Serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
