Nachos

Description

Nachos are a beloved Mexican dish that consists of tortilla chips topped with melted cheese and a variety of flavorful toppings. Perfect for a snack, appetizer, or even a meal, nachos are highly customizable and always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of tortilla chips
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cooked ground beef or shredded chicken (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapeños
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup guacamole
  • 1 cup salsa
Steps:
  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Assemble the Nachos: On a large baking sheet, spread out an even layer of tortilla chips. Sprinkle half of the
  3. shredded cheese over the chips. Top with black beans, cooked ground beef or chicken (if using), black olives,
  4. diced tomatoes, jalapeños, and green onions. Sprinkle the remaining cheese on top.
  5. Bake: Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is fully melted and
  6. bubbly.
  7. Add Fresh Toppings: Remove the nachos from the oven and sprinkle with fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve: Serve immediately with sour cream, guacamole, and salsa on the side.
